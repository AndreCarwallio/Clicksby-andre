About

I’m Andre, a Grand Valley State University student with a real passion for photography. What started as something creative on the side quickly turned into helping friends, classmates, and campus organizations capture moments that actually look professional.

My focus is simple: clean edits, sharp images, and affordable pricing — especially for students and campus groups. Whether it’s headshots, club events, or personal shoots, I aim to make the process easy and stress-free while delivering photos you’ll actually want to post.

This isn’t a big studio — it’s personal, flexible, and built around what you need.
